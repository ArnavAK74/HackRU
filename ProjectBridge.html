Project Inspiration: I-40 Bridge Disaster

Overview

The I-40 Bridge Disaster occurred on May 26, 2002, when a barge collided with a bridge pier near Webbers Falls, Oklahoma, causing a section of the bridge to collapse. This tragic event resulted in the loss of 14 lives and highlighted the importance of bridge safety and integrity.
